For as long as I can remember, I've been fascinated by how things connect. As a kid, it was connecting facts to win quiz competitions for my school. Today, it's connecting systems to help businesses work smarter, not just harder.

My journey into technology began over a decade ago with a deep dive into full-stack web development. I started by building the foundations of the web, working extensively with PHP, Drupal, WordPress, and managing applications on Apache servers. This taught me how to build robust, reliable digital experiences from the ground up.

As the digital world evolved, so did my craft. I embraced the modern web, mastering the JavaScript ecosystem—including Node.js, React, Astro, and TypeScript—to build complex, progressive web applications and design seamless user experiences. This phase was all about building and experimenting: carrying projects from initial branding and UI/UX design all the way to shipping optimizations on production codebases.

## The Shift to Intelligent Automation

After years of building custom solutions, I saw a recurring pattern: brilliant founders and businesses getting bogged down by manual, repetitive tasks that stifled their growth. I realized that the biggest value I could provide wasn't just building another app, but architecting systems that could run themselves.

That insight changed everything.

Today, my focus is on designing and implementing intelligent automation systems. I use my deep technical background to integrate secure AI models and powerful platforms like n8n into your existing workflows. My passion for open standards like OpenAPI helps me create seamless connections between your tools, eliminating bottlenecks and unlocking new efficiencies.

Whether it's architecting on cloud platforms like AWS and GCP or contributing to the latest in Deno, my love for learning keeps me at the forefront of what's possible.

Think of me as a systems architect with a developer's toolkit and a designer's eye. I can help you with everything from high-level automation strategy to the hands-on technical implementation. If you're ready to move from manual processes to intelligent, scalable workflows, I'd love to connect.

**Let's build a more efficient future for your business, together.**
